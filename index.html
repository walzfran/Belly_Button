Overview

To use this website, please select a test subject that you would like to view from the "Test Subject ID" dropdown. Once an ID has been selected the demographic information will be displayed as well as the population of 3 infographics. The first one displayed is a gauge that shows how frequently the subject cleans thier belly button. The second is a bar chart that shows the top 10 bacterias found in the sample and the bottom image shows the total cultures in bubble with the largest being the most prevalent.

Enjoy!
